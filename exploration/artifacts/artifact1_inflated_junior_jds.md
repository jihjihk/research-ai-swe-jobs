# Artifact 1: Scope-Inflated Entry-Level SWE Job Descriptions (March 2026)

## Purpose
These are real LinkedIn SWE job postings from March 2026, classified as entry-level by the
platform's seniority label, that contain an unusually high density of organizational-complexity
language. In our data, entry-level postings in 2026 show +10pp in "cross-functional" mentions,
+8pp in "ownership" mentions, and +24pp in "collaboration" mentions compared to 2024 entry-level
postings (T12 finding). These exemplars represent the upper tail of that shift.

## Interview prompt
"Here is a real entry-level software engineering job posting from LinkedIn in 2026. What stands
out to you about the expectations described? Would this have been posted as an entry-level role
two years ago? Why or why not?"
