## How to use in interviews

Each artifact can be printed or displayed on screen during a semi-structured interview.
The interview protocol should:

1. Present the artifact without leading interpretation
2. Ask the interviewee what they notice or find surprising
3. Probe whether their own experience aligns with or contradicts the pattern
4. Ask about mechanisms: why do they think this shift happened?
5. Explore consequences: what does this mean for junior engineers? for hiring managers?
