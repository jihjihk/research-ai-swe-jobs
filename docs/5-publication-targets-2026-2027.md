# Publication Targets: 2026-2027

This file tracks candidate submission venues for the dataset paper and the substantive research paper. It is an execution-planning document, not part of the research design itself.

Verified against official conference pages on 2026-03-20. Two publishable outputs:
1. **Research paper** — employer-side restructuring of SWE roles, junior scope inflation, senior archetype shift, and employer-requirement / worker-usage divergence
2. **Dataset paper** — longitudinal US SWE job-postings dataset with canonical-posting and observation-level outputs
