# New-angle scan — consulting, analyst, VC, Big-Tech (2024-26)

Ten quantitative, testable claims going BEYOND our 8 existing pieces (junior/senior reshape, Copilot codification, AI geography, finance-AI density, unvalidated regex, rejected alts, Applied-AI YOE, rank concordance).

## HIGH INTEREST

### C1. "Dev time-to-fill doubles in 2026" — Forrester
- **Source**: Forrester, *Predictions 2026: AI Moves From Hype To Hard Hat Work* (Nov 2025).
- **Claim**: Firms demand senior architecture + AI fluency; time-to-fill doubles.
- **Type**: Forecast.
- **Test**: Posting-age (days-listed) × seniority × year. Widening senior-minus-junior age gap?
- **Vs our 8**: Extends piece 1 (junior/senior) with a new axis — duration, not volume.
- **Headline**: "The senior listings that won't close."

### C2. "Juniors 7-10% SLOWER with AI tools" — McKinsey
- **Source**: McKinsey, *Unleashing developer productivity with gen AI*, 2023 + 2024/25 updates.
- **Claim**: <1-yr devs took 7-10% LONGER on some tasks with AI; savings <10% on high-complexity.
- **Type**: Data-led (experimental).
- **Test**: Did AI-skill mentions grow slower at entry level than senior level? Cross-tab AI-skills × seniority × year.
- **Vs our 8**: Supplies the mechanism behind piece 1.
- **Headline**: "Why juniors got squeezed: the McKinsey 7-10% rule."

### C3. "90% of enterprise SWEs on AI code assistants by 2028" — Gartner (revised up from 75%)
- **Source**: Gartner 2024 release (75%/2028) → revised to 90%/2028 in 2025.
- **Claim**: 90% by 2028, up from <14% early 2024.
- **Test**: Share of postings REQUIRING Copilot/Cursor/Claude Code/Windsurf/Cline, Mar 2024 → Mar 2026.
- **Vs our 8**: Agrees with piece 2. Flag gap: "used" vs "formalized as hiring criterion."
- **Headline**: "Gartner said 75%, then 90%. Here's what the ads actually require."

### C4. "Platform-engineering: 55% → 80% of large orgs by end-2026" — Gartner
- **Source**: Gartner, *Strategic Trends in Platform Engineering 2025*.
- **Claim**: 80% adoption by 2026; DevOps/SRE absorbed into platform eng.
- **Test**: Growth of "platform engineer" vs "DevOps" vs "SRE" vs "full-stack" titles. Platform >2x full-stack? DevOps/SRE plateau?
- **Vs our 8**: ORTHOGONAL — no title-bucket comparison in our 8.
- **Headline**: "The platform-engineer boom: testing Gartner's 80%-by-2026 claim."

### C5. "AI-skills wage premium: 25% → 56% in one year" — PwC
- **Source**: PwC, *Fearless Future: 2025 Global AI Jobs Barometer*, ~1bn ads.
- **Claim**: Premium doubled YoY; skills-change velocity 66% faster in AI-exposed roles.
- **Test**: For postings with salary bands, compare AI-skill-mentioned vs not within same title/metro/seniority. Widening 2024-2026?
- **Vs our 8**: ORTHOGONAL. No salary angle in our 8.
- **Headline**: "The 56% premium, visible in ours."

### C6. "SWE is the leading scaled agentic-AI function" — McKinsey State of AI 2025
- **Source**: McKinsey QuantumBlack, *State of AI 2025: Agents, Innovation, Transformation* (Nov 2025).
- **Claim**: 23% of orgs scaling agents; SWE highest-scaled function; tech industry leads.
- **Test**: Postings mentioning "agents/agentic/MCP/tool-use" × industry × function. Tech-SWE > finance-SWE?
- **Vs our 8**: Refines piece 7 — specifies *agentic* as rising sub-archetype.
- **Headline**: "Agentic engineers: the first role to scale."

## MEDIUM INTEREST

### C7. "30% of new code at Google is AI-generated" — Pichai, Q1 2025
- **Source**: Alphabet earnings, confirmed Q3 2025 (25% → 30%).
- **Test**: Do Alphabet postings de-emphasize "write code" and emphasize "review/architect/validate"? Verb-frequency × company × year.
- **Vs our 8**: Extends piece 2 indirectly.
- **Headline**: "30% of the code is machine-written. Is 30% of the job ad?"

### C8. "Klarna −47% headcount while raising pay 60%" + Shopify/Amazon AI-first memos
- **Source**: CNBC May 2025; Lütke memo Apr 2025; Jassy memo Jun 2025 ("reduce total corporate workforce").
- **Test**: SWE-posting volume per memo signatory (Shopify/Amazon/Klarna/Meta) vs industry peers, 2024-2026.
- **Vs our 8**: ORTHOGONAL — no memo-cohort slice in our 8.
- **Headline**: "The AI-first memo cohort: postings fell 2x faster."

### C9. ">50% of enterprise gen-AI goes domain-specific by 2028" + "domain > coding" — Gartner
- **Source**: Gartner 2026 Strategic Predictions (Oct 2025).
- **Test**: Growth of vertical SWE titles ("healthcare/fintech/legal-tech/bio-SWE") vs pure-tech titles.
- **Vs our 8**: Piece 4 (finance-AI) is a seed; C9 generalizes to all verticals.
- **Headline**: "The generic full-stack is dying. Domain-SWE is rising."

### C10. "44% of US work hours automatable via agents today" — MGI Nov 2025
- **Source**: McKinsey Global Institute, *Agents, Robots, and Us* (Nov 2025).
- **Claim**: 57% total automatable, 44% via agents; SWE in top-4 gen-AI value pools.
- **Test**: Automatable task-verbs (CRUD/boilerplate/test scaffolding) decline faster than architecture/system-design mentions in postings.
- **Vs our 8**: Extends piece 5 — we have the regex framework; add task-verb taxonomy.
- **Headline**: "The 44% ceiling: which SWE tasks are evaporating from ads."
