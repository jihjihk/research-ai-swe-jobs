# Artifact 2: Paired Job Descriptions -- Same Company, 2024 vs 2026

## Purpose
These are real SWE job posting pairs from the same company, one posted in April 2024
(arshkon dataset) and one posted in March 2026 (scraped). T13 found that within-company
entry-level share declined by 9.3 percentage points, AI keyword prevalence surged by
+33pp within overlapping companies, and description length increased by 1.43x.

## Interview prompt
"Here are two software engineering job postings from the same company, one from 2024 and
one from 2026. What differences do you notice in what the employer is looking for? Has
your own experience of hiring or being hired changed in similar ways?"
