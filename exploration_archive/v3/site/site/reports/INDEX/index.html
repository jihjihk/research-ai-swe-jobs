    Key findings after Gate 3

    What IS strong and SWE-specific (paper core)

    1. AI requirements surge: 8%→33% (DiD vs control: +24.4pp). Validated as genuine, not aspirational.
    2. Junior share decline: SWE-specific (DiD = -24.9pp; control junior share increased).
    3. Domain recomposition: ML/AI 4%→27%, Frontend 41%→24%.
    4. AI is additive: 11.4 vs 7.3 techs per posting. New 25-tech AI community emerged.
    5. YOE slot purification: 5+ YOE entry: 22.8%→2.4%. Genuine, within-title.
    6. GenAI accelerated 8.3x between within-2024 and cross-period rates.

    What is NOT SWE-specific or was corrected

    1. Management indicator CORRECTED: +31pp was measurement error → actual +4-10pp, AND field-wide (DiD ~0).
    2. Soft skills expansion: SWE grew LESS than control (DiD = -5.1pp).
    3. AI-entry orthogonality: r=-0.07 (firm), r=-0.04 (metro). Parallel trends, not causal.
    4. 57% of aggregate change is compositional (different companies), not behavioral.
    5. Requirements LAG usage (~41% vs ~75%), inverting RQ3's hypothesis. Gap narrowing.

    Seniority structure

    1. Associate collapsing toward entry (relative position 0.30→0.16). 5-level → ~3 tiers.
    2. Senior orchestration shift: Director management density fell 23%, orchestration surged 46%.
    3. Management expanded at ALL levels — not migration from senior to entry.
