## Core Narrative

Between 2024 and 2026, SWE and software-adjacent technical postings do not show a robust junior-role collapse. The stronger evidence is that postings broadened around AI/tool/platform/workflow requirements across returning companies, metros, and adjacent technical occupations, while seniority labels and YOE floors diverged as measurement signals.
