    SWE Labor Market Exploration (2024 → 2026)¶

    Between 2024 and 2026, US LinkedIn software-engineering postings added AI-tool vocabulary at a rate 99 percent specific to SWE versus control occupations, 102 percent attributable to the same 240 companies rewriting their own postings, while employers under-specified AI by 15–30 percentage points versus developer usage.

    The rewrite is cross-seniority, cross-archetype, geographically uniform. It coexists with a senior-disproportionate shift toward mentoring and orchestration, and a sharpening (not blurring) of seniority boundaries.
